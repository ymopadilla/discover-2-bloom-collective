Transition Coaching for Your Next Chapter

We help people develop the psychological architecture that research shows actually predicts success through change. This isn't motivation. This isn't cheerleading. This is building the internal infrastructure that allows you to thrive through transition.

Start Your Transformation

We all carry a vision of what our lives "should" look like by now—the career milestone, the relationship, the sense of arrival. When reality doesn't match that picture, we get stuck. Not because we've failed, but because we're measuring against a bloom that was never meant to be ours.

What if the path forward isn't about reaching that original vision—but reimagining what your bloom is supposed to look like now, with everything you've learned and lived?

That's the work we do together.

Philosophy

You are built to blossom into more

Most people aren't built for quick pivots—and that's normal. That's why coaching exists. The ability to navigate change can be learned and developed.

My approach integrates insights from leading researchers—Angela Duckworth's work on grit, James Clear on habits, Brené Brown on resilience, Stephen Covey's principles, and the latest neuroplasticity science—into a practical framework that meets you exactly where you are.

You have everything you need—you just need someone who'll tell you the truth and walk beside you while you prove it to yourself.

The Kintsugi Philosophy

The Japanese art of Kintsugi repairs broken pottery with gold, making the cracks visible and beautiful rather than hiding them. Someone who has been broken and healed can become more beautiful and more lovable than ever before.

We are each other's wounded healers. Your story—your transitions, your struggles, your growth—is your credential. The gold is already in you.

What I Believe

  • Your strongest bloom is still ahead of you.
  • Transition is evolution, not failure.
  • No excuses, just choices.
  • You are built not to shrink down to less but to blossom into more.

Who I Help

Career change. Empty nest. Loss. Retirement. Graduation. Divorce. Starting over.

Whatever transition has you feeling stuck, I'll help you find your path forward.
