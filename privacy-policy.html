Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

Discover to Bloom Collective ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website discover2bloom.com and use our coaching and retreat services.

Our Commitment: We believe in transparency and respect for your personal information. We collect only what we need to serve you well, and we never sell your data.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide to Us

We collect information you voluntarily provide when you:

  • Fill out contact forms or inquiry forms on our website
  • Subscribe to our newsletter or email list
  • Register for a Discovery Map Retreat or coaching services
  • Communicate with us via email, phone, or other channels
  • Complete intake forms or assessments as part of our coaching process

This information may include:

  • Name and contact information (email address, phone number, mailing address)
  • Payment and billing information (processed securely through Stripe)
  • Information you share during coaching sessions or retreat experiences
  • Any other information you choose to provide

Information Collected Automatically

When you visit our website, we may automatically collect certain technical information, including:

  • Browser type and version
  • Device type
  • Pages visited and time spent on pages
  • Referring website or source

We do not currently use Google Analytics or third-party tracking cookies.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our coaching and retreat services
  • Process registrations and payments for our services
  • Send you newsletters, updates, and promotional materials (only if you've opted in)
  • Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
  • Schedule and manage coaching sessions and retreat logistics
  • Comply with legal obligations

How We Share Your Information

We respect your privacy and limit sharing of your information. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

Payment Processing

We use Stripe to process payments securely. When you make a payment, your payment information is transmitted directly to Stripe and is subject to their Privacy Policy. We do not store your full credit card information on our servers.

Service Providers

We may share information with trusted service providers who assist us in operating our website and conducting our business (such as email service providers for newsletters), provided they agree to keep your information confidential.

Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or governmental regulation, or if we believe disclosure is necessary to protect our rights, your safety, or the safety of others.

We Do Not Sell Your Data. We will never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

Email Communications

If you subscribe to our newsletter or email list, you will receive periodic emails about:

  • Upcoming retreats and coaching opportunities
  • Resources, articles, and insights related to personal transformation
  • Updates about Discover to Bloom Collective

You can unsubscribe from our email list at any time by clicking the "unsubscribe" link at the bottom of any email or by contacting us directly at team@discover2bloom.com.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Correct any inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Delete your personal information (subject to certain legal exceptions)
  • Opt out of marketing communications at any time
  • Request a copy of your data in a portable format

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at team@discover2bloom.com.

Confidentiality of Coaching Relationships

We take the confidentiality of our coaching relationships seriously. Information shared during coaching sessions and retreats is kept strictly confidential, except:

  • When you provide written consent to share specific information
  • When required by law
  • When there is risk of harm to yourself or others

Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for individuals 18 years of age and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If you believe we have collected information from a minor, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.
